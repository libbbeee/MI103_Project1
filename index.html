Root

Root is a board game made by the company Leder Games. It is an asymmetrical game where everyone plays different woodland factions and fight to rule over the forest.

In the base game, there are four options for players to choose from when deciding their faction.

Marquise de Cat

Collect wood to build infrastructure in the woodland, which lets you grow your army and craft more supplies.

Eyrie Dynasty

Capture territories and build roost to lead you kingdom of birds to victory.

Woodland Alliance

Garner sympathy from woodland citizens to revolt against the stronger militant factions.

Vagabond

Work alone in the shadows to complete quests and explore the woodland.
